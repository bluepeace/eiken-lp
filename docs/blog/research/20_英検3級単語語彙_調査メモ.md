## 3. 出題傾向・単語の特徴

- **場面・題材**：身近なこと。家庭、学校、地域、電話、アナウンス。人物紹介、歴史、海外の文化なども出る。
- **ライティング・面接**：理由を述べるときに使う語（because, so, think, want, like, good, interesting など）を押さえるとよい。
- **頻出とされる語の例**（複数ソースで挙がるもの）：actually, certainly, enough, favorite, forget, recently, already, yet, experience, culture, foreign, popular, decide, suggest

---

## 4. 覚えた方がよい単語一覧（例・カテゴリ別）

※ 公式リストではなく、**中学卒業程度でよく使われる語の代表例**です。

### 副詞・つなぎの語
actually, certainly, recently, already, yet, still, perhaps, maybe, finally, suddenly, especially, usually, always, never

### 動詞（4級に加えて）
forget, remember, decide, suggest, expect, allow, encourage, discuss, explain, describe, improve, continue, happen, seem, become

### 形容詞
favorite, enough, certain, recent, foreign, popular, traditional, modern, natural, necessary, possible, different, same, important

### 名詞
experience, culture, history, reason, result, change, chance, idea, opinion, problem, question, way, kind, part, place, time

### 接続詞・表現
because, so that, although, whether, that（接続詞）, It is ～ to …, I think that …, want to, try to, need to

### フレーズの例
for example, such as, a lot of, one of the ～, be interested in, be good at, take care of
